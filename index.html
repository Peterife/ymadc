2021 Impact Story

With a firm focus on holistic and transformational education, we rolled out a series of projects targeted at young people from diverse backgrounds across various communities. Our goal was to strategically empower 1,800 youth by December 2021. Over the course of the year, we implemented a range of initiatives, including seminars, advocacy campaigns, workshops, camps, and a dedicated Workplace Skills Centre. Through these programs, we aimed to equip young people with the necessary skills, values, and knowledge to thrive in the world of tomorrow. As the year progressed, we began to see the impact of our work. Young people who had participated in our programs were taking on leadership roles in their communities, initiating projects of their own, and demonstrating a newfound sense of confidence and purpose. By December 2021, we had successfully empowered over 1,800 young people from diverse communities. We were heartened by the positive feedback we received from participants, and we were proud to have made a tangible difference in their lives. Moving forward, we remain committed to our mission of providing transformative education and empowering the next generation of African leaders.
